Hi, I'm Matt Paulin — a product leader, technologist, and entrepreneur based in Seattle.

I've spent 20 years founding companies, shipping software, and working alongside the people building things that matter. I love difficult, multi-faceted problems with many degrees of freedom — the kind where the answer isn't obvious until you've built something and learned from it.

I'm a pragmatic optimist who cares about making technology more accessible, more equitable, and more human. I believe the best products are the ones that give people new ways to understand and act in the world.

My consulting practice is **[Ministry of Product](https://ministryofproduct.com)**, where I work with founders and executives as an embedded product partner — taking ideas from vision to working software.

I write here about product development, AI, and the thinking behind the work. The posts range from frameworks I use in practice to experiments I'm running to observations about how software actually gets built.
